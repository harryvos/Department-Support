During Alpha, GDS can help you to build effective prototypes, understand how to test prototypes with users, and assess the technical feasibility of building your proposed services.

Prototyping

In Alpha, prototypes help you test out different ways a service might meet user needs identified in Discovery.

GDS have published guidance about prototyping in the Service Manual:

  • How your alpha prototype should look – The purpose of prototypes in Alpha
  • Making prototypes – When to use different types of prototypes, such as sketch or code prototypes

If you or your team are struggling with making prototypes, the GDS Design Training covers the basics of prototyping, focusing on using the GOV.UK Prototype Kit. To book yourself or your team onto the monthly three day training, send Rosie Clayton an email to find out when the next available course is.

Testing prototypes with users

You should always test your prototypes with users, to test your assumptions, and evaluate how well you are meeting your users' needs.

GDS have published guidance about usability testing in the Service Manual:

  • Using moderated usability testing
  • User research in Alpha

If you are new to user research, or coming from a background in design, quality assurance, or other research disciplines, GDS run an introduction and basic training days for user research for government services.

Assessing technical feasibility

During Alpha, you should have a technical lead who can assess the feasibility of building the prototypes you make at scale, using production code and live technical architecture. Often, the proposed service will have to interface with other existing systems and services, where there may be legacy constraints you will need to consider as you design your service.

These feasibility studies will help you decide whether progressing to Private Beta will be possible, and which service design is most appropriate to take forwards, based primarily on user research, but also the feasibility of setting up the technology infrastructure.

If you are struggling with particularly complex systems, a session with one of the GDS Consulting Technical Architects. Contact Ola Bamgboye to arrange a session.
